Button

A useful element with multiple ways to customize through CSS classes.



Usually a framework includes 5 colors for the button element. These colors are primary, info, success, warning and danger. Sometimes they also include a dark, a default and even a secondary. SocialiteUI comes with those and also with a brand color.
